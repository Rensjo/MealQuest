Your daily command center — meal slots, health indicator, trackers, charts, and Smart Intelligence widgets in a bento-grid layout.

🍽 4 Meal Slots

Color-coded Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Snack cards with live calorie totals and meal log summaries.

📊 Health Indicator

Analyzes 7-day and 30-day meal balance. Rates your habit: Great, Good, Fair, Poor, or Critical.

💧 Water & Sweet Trackers

Animated SVG progress rings. Quick-add buttons (250ml, 500ml). Separate tracking for water, sugary drinks, and alcohol.

📈 Calorie Trend & Macro Charts

Recharts line chart for daily calorie history with goal line overlay. Pie chart for protein / carbs / fat distribution.

🗓 Meal Heatmap

7-day or 30-day calendar showing meal-count intensity per day — instantly see logging consistency at a glance.

Page 2 — Meal Log
Log Meals in Seconds

Type a food name, select your portion, and auto-estimated nutrition is filled in instantly — no manual lookup required.

🔍 Smart Food Search

Type any food name → real-time autocomplete dropdown from the built-in food database. No internet needed.

⚖️ Portion Presets

One click to set portion size: Small, Medium, Large, Extra Large. Nutrition scales automatically.

🏠 Food-Source Tagging

Label each meal: Home-Cooked · Fast-Food · Dine-Out · Street-Food · Processed. Tracked for health analytics.

📅 Week View Navigation

7-day mini calendar at the top. Navigate to any day; visual meal-count badges show how many meals were logged on each day.

✏️ Manual Override

Edit any auto-estimated value directly — calories, protein, carbs, fat, water. Full control when you need it.

📖 Recipe Vault

  • Create recipes with name, ingredients, macros, prep/cook time
  • Filter by All / Favorites / Quick Recipes
  • Per-recipe: calories, protein, carbs, fat macro cards
  • Tag-based search and favorite toggle

📦 Pantry Manager

  • Inventory with quantity, unit, category, and expiry date
  • Low-stock alerts highlighted in yellow
  • ± quantity adjustments and expiry warnings
  • Auto-syncs when grocery items are marked purchased

🛒 Grocery List

  • Add items with quantity, unit, store, price, and priority
  • Real-time total cost calculation
  • Toggle purchased → one-click syncs to Pantry
  • Clear all purchased items with one action

Page 4 — Nutrition Goals
Set Targets. Pick a Strategy. Hit Goals.

Five diet strategy presets auto-sync your macro targets. Watch real-time progress rings update as you log meals throughout the day.

⚡ Diet Strategy Presets

🎯Balanced

⚡Performance

Each preset applies recommended macros instantly. Active strategy grants an XP multiplier (up to +15%).

🎯 Editable Targets

  • Set custom Calories, Protein (g), Carbs (g), Fat (g), Water (ml)
  • Defaults: 2000 kcal · 150g protein · 250g carbs · 65g fat · 2500ml
  • Values persist across sessions via Zustand persist

💍 Real-time Progress Rings

Animated SVG rings per macro — update live as meals are logged. Shows current value, 7-day rolling average, and % met today.

See how your past week trends against each goal. "Goals Met Today" badge in top-right corner counts how many targets you've hit.

🏆 Nutrition Score

Daily 0–100 score with S–F grade. 5 weighted categories: Meal Consistency (25), Nutrition Balance (30), Hydration (20), Sugar Control (15), Home-Cooked (10).

💡 Weekly Insights Engine

Auto-detects patterns across 14+ days of data — protein trends, hydration dips, breakfast consistency, calorie streaks — with actionable tips for each.

🧠 Habit Pattern Detection

Identifies positive and negative eating habits. Green = positive, Red = negative, Yellow = neutral. Each habit shows impact rating.

🍽 Meal Suggestion Engine

Scores recipes by diet strategy alignment, pantry ingredient availability, and meal diversity. Shows top suggestions with reasons.

🛒 Predictive Grocery

Forecasts what you'll need to buy based on past consumption frequency and current pantry stock. Shows confidence % per item.

📈 Monthly Trend Chart

12-month Recharts area chart of nutrition score and meal consistency. Gradient fill with hover tooltips.
